Artificial Intelligence (AI ) is the ability of a computer-controlled computer or robot to perform tasks usually performed by humans, as they require human intelligence and discernment.
Even though there are no AIs capable of performing the wide variety of tasks that an ordinary human can perform, some AIs can match people in specific tasks.
