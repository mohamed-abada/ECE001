Artificial Intelligence can be categorized into different groups, with two primary categorization categories basing on skills and functionally based on AI.
Following is a flow diagram describing the AI forms.


The first type is based on Capabilities:

  1. Weak AI or Narrow AI

    2. Narrow AI is a type of AI that can perform a dedicated task with intelligence. The most popular and currently accessible AI in the
    Artificial Intelligence environment is Narrow AI.

  2. General AI

    3. General AI is a type of intelligence which, like a human, could perform any intellectual task with efficiency.

  3. Super AI

    4. Super AI is a system intelligence level where computers can exceed human intelligence and perform any function with cognitive
    properties better than humans. This is a General AI result.
The second type is based on functionality:

  1. Reactive Machines

    2. 1-Purely reactive machines are the most basic types of Artificial Intelligence.
    2-These AI systems do not store memories for potential acts, or past experiences.

  2. Limited Memory

    3. 1-Limited memory machines can store, for a short time, past experiences or some data.
    2-One of the best examples of Limited Memory systems is the self-driving cars. Such cars can store nearby cars' recent speed,
    other car distance, speed limit, and other information required to navigate the route.

  3. Theory of Mind

    4. 1-Theory of mind AI should consider human emotions, personalities, values and be able to interact socially like humans.
    2-There is still no development of this type of AI machines, but researchers are making a lot of effort and progress for developing these AI machines.

  4. Self-Awareness

    5. 1-The future of Artificial Intelligence is Self-awareness AI. These machines will be super smart, and will have their own awareness, feelings, and self-awareness.
    2-In truth, AI Self-Awareness still does not exist and is a hypothetical term.
